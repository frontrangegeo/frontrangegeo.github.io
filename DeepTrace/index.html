DeepTrace

A.I. for first break picking

DeepTrace is a breakthrough application of deep learning for picking arrival times in seismic data. Integrated with FRG's new Phoenix Refraction Statics program, DeepTrace can pick billions of traces per hour at human quality. Users can train further train DeepTrace on their historical data to continually refine DeepTrace's picking abilities in diverse geophysical contexts.
