Steps

  1. Define SEGY Dictionary
  2. Launch Import Job

Define SEGY Dictionary

Following the SPS standard (found at seg.org standards web page) shots and stations are uniquely identified by three numbers - LineNumber, PointNumber, and PointIndex.

The following information must be available if the SEGY trace headers:

  • Shot PointNumber (optional - PointIndex and LineNumber)
  • Shot Easting, Northing, Elevation (optional - WaterDepth, PointDepth, Uphole)
  • Station PointNumber (optional - PointIndex and LineNumber)
  • Station Easting, Northing, Elevation (optional - WaterDepth, PointDepth, Uphole)
  • Trace
